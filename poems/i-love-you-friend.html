I Love You, Friend

Oct 2, 2015
Come now with me and walk a while,
You need not talk, but merely smile.
I like your look, your way, your style,
Don't put me through a lengthy trial.
The world is full, with much to see,
And by your side I wish to be.
The paths ahead are new to me,
But we to travel them are free.
Alone I've come a distance great,
I hope I haven't come too late.
I think I'm rather sent by fate
That we might share this joyful state.
Your beauty does with nature blend,
My heart to paradise does send.
Forever you, would I defend
Because, you see, I love you, friend.
— Thayne Whipple
