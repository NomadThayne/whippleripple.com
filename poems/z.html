A Poem by Thayne Whipple

Z

Apr 20, 2016
We've Zoomed right through the alphabet,
Some questions though remain.
Do Zebras eat Zucchini in
The Zoo out in the rain?
You guessed quite right, the letter left
To introduce is Z.
And now it's done I thank you all
For spending time with me!
— Thayne Whipple
