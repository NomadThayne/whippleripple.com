A Journey Through Hell

Feb 8, 2016
When on a path that leads through hell,
Don't stop and sit to rest a spell.
If on your problems you don't dwell,
In time, you'll see, they'll turn out well.
When in the throes of hell you're found,
Keep moving on to higher ground.
Where you can clearly look around,
And see your blessings do abound.
When hope is lost and friends are gone -
Your life is hell ... keep moving on.
And as you fight with brain and brawn,
You'll surely see a brighter dawn.
Each one of us has felt some pain,
And had a hard time staying sane.
Do not succumb to stress and strain.
You've everything in life to gain!
Inspired by:
"If you're going through hell, keep going."
- Winston Churchill
— Thayne Whipple
