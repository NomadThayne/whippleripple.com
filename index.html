About the Author
Thayne Whipple
A poet, storyteller, and author who divides his time between Ojai, California and Laʻie, Hawaiʻi. His writing spans the lyrical and the playful — from sea-washed sonnets and sacred ballads to wry observations on modern life. With six published collections, his work has delighted readers across generations.
